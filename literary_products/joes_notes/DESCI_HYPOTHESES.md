### Decentralized Science ([DeSci](/literary_products/joes_notes/DESCI.md)) Hypotheses



1. **Big-cap corporation** [valuations](/literary_products/joes_notes/VALUATIONS.md) have surpassed [trillions of dollars](/literary_products/joes_notes/TRILLION_DOLLARS.md). If [cryptocurrencies](/literary_products/joes_notes/CRYPTOCURRENCIES.md) and [tokens](/literary_products/joes_notes/TOKENS.md) are accepted by [structural economic institutions](/literary_products/joes_notes/STRUCTURAL_ECONOMIC_INSTITUTIONS.md) such as [pension funds](/literary_products/joes_notes/PENSION_FUNDS.md) and [sovereign wealth funds](/literary_products/joes_notes/SOVEREIGN_WEALTH_FUNDS.md), [DeSci organizations](/literary_products/joes_notes/DESCI_ORGANIZATIONS.md) stand to realize the same [valuations](/literary_products/joes_notes/VALUATIONS.md). However, current early-stage [DeSci](/literary_products/joes_notes/DESCI.md) organizations must “[position](/literary_products/joes_notes/POSITIONING_STRATEGIES.md)” ([corporate, legal structural compatibility](/literary_products/joes_notes/CORPORATE_LEGAL_STRUCTURES.md)) themselves now.

2. [Medical and health sciences](/literary_products/joes_notes/MEDICAL_AND_HEALTH_SCIENCES.md) require billions of [patient](/literary_products/joes_notes/PATIENT_DATA.md) participants in large-scale [digital therapeutic](/literary_products/joes_notes/DIGITAL_THERAPEUTICS.md) studies (unprecedented but “[required](/literary_products/joes_notes/REQUIREMENTS_IN_HEALTH_STUDIES.md)”). These are only feasible via [DeSci](/literary_products/joes_notes/DESCI.md) approaches that entail [open-source coordination](/literary_products/joes_notes/OPEN_SOURCE_COORDINATION.md) and [efficiency](/literary_products/joes_notes/EFFICIENCY_METRICS.md).

3. To facilitate accelerated progress in [science](/literary_products/joes_notes/SCIENCE_LANDSCAPE.md), a global, universal [citation chain](/literary_products/joes_notes/CITATION_CHAIN.md) of [open-source science resources](/literary_products/joes_notes/OPEN_SOURCE_SCIENCE_RESOURCES.md) ([science's counterpart to open educational resources](/literary_products/joes_notes/OER_COUNTERPART.md) or [OERs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open_educational_resources)) is required.
